Our Testimonials

Delvin Lee

43   Senior Producer

I started to get heavier when I was reaching my mid 30s, and could feel myself getting lethargic easily.

For the past ten years, I have been trying to lose weight and get healthier by exercising and going gym. But I did not lose weight.

I started exercising with Revvo in Aug 2021, and as of May 2022, I have lost almost 9 kg. That's something I have never dreamed would be possible, epecially at this age. I got a happy problem when my pants and tee shirts were much looser now. In fact, I had to buy new pants, very timely as I had to go back to working in the office as work life was back to pre-Covid days.

Besides losing weight, one of the most important benefits I have obtained is that I now know some key ways in how to maintain a healthy lifestyle, and becoming healthier and fitter. I acquired this knowledge from Revvo Coaches who were very generous and passionate in sharing tips on eating right, with occasional indulgence, and how to sustain an active lifestyle.

To me, Revvo is very different from other gyms and studios, all coaches and members relate to each other as family and friends, who share similar mindsets of being healthy and happy for life, for self and loved ones.

I find Revvo Coaches very personable and approachable. They will customise the training to each individual's needs and capabilities, and they will also push the individual to do more if he is more than ready, but never at the expense of personal injury or over-exertion. They know their stuff well - the correct postures to do the various exercises and to get the most out of it, and they will constantly follow up with you to help you achieve your goals.

Get immersed in Revvo environment. I believe you will achieve your weight, health and fitness goals in a fun and effective manner, making a few friends here and there, just like me.
