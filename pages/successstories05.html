Our Testimonials

Eugene Seah

46   Life Coach

As an Life Coach, I have to stand in front of my participants to facilitate and engage them during the training and workshops. It is important for me to dress sharp and to have the energy to send positive vibes and be animated to hold their focus.

I was not that very oversized to begin with. But due to a hectic lifestyle and poor eating habits, I started to have a protruding tummy. Soon, I was dissatisfied with the way I looked in slim fit business shirts. I tried exercising to get rid of the tummy, but it did not work.

Thanks to my Revvo Coach, who was passionate in educating me on the science behind weight loss, which is majorly about food intake, and also got me to join the Revvo community for the workout sessions, I got my desired result within 1 month! My slim fit shirts were “fitting” again. I knew I had achieved something when my workshop participants told me that I looked good in the shirt and jacket!

Working out at Revvo is something that I look forward to. The coaches are friendly, always ensuring that the participants have fun, full of smiles and yet they have a great effective “sweat-out” workout session. Although all are group-based workouts, the coaches are able to personalise each exercise to the fitness and functional level of each individual. This ensures that all workouts are able to meet each individual’s needs, comfort level and pace.

Effectiveness, safety, assurance and fun are the key elements found here.

I would think that “Revvo doesn’t sell sweat, Revvo sells Results”.

I must say that Revvo is a great partner to have in my lifelong wellness journey.
