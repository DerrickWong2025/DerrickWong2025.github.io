Pillars of Our Programmes

Fitness

More than 22 group workout sessions are provided per week. All workout routines are carefully designed to engage, strengthen and tone up the body as well as developing the cardio aspects. While the fitter participants are able to do the workouts at more intense pace or with heavier weights, at the same time, we are able to cater to the not-as-fit by introducing regression or less intense versions.

Nutrition

To achieve health and fitness results, nutrition plays a very critical role. It is not only about the controlling of macro nutrients which is the key focus for weight management and muscle gain, it is also about the micronutrients and phytonutrients that support the absorption of the macros, as well as to develop immunity and enhance cell regeneration. Education, engagement and experiential support are provided to cover this highly important aspect.

Coaches and Community

It is very challenging to be on a solo journey to achieve weight, health and fitness transformation. The journey will be made easier and fun for you when you have credible Revvo coaches who can inspire you and when you are immersed in a community of people with the similar mindset and great enthusiasm for a healthier, fitter and happier life.

The RITE Workouts for you

REVVO Interval Training Experience (RITE) is a 45 min workout session that caters to people of all fitness and functional levels. Whether you start from ground zero or one who is conditioned for high performance workout, we guarantee you a GOOD SWEATOUT! All exercises with effective results end with nourishing yourself with a post workout recovery protein shake.

Our Process

A

Getting to Know You

To find out your goals regarding weight, health and fitness and your lifestyle

B

Intervention

To propose a programme to help you achieve your goals in a realistic, sustainable pace, and in a healthy manner.

Choice of programme

  • Fitness only — Group Workouts or Personal Trainer
  • Fitness and Nutrition with Coach Support

C

Implementation, Tracking & Review

For programmes that involve Personal Trainer or Coach Support, the assigned PT or Coach will work with you to develop and implement a detailed plan on how you want to be supported and coached for results. This plan will cover:

  • Workout sessions you are attending
  • Scheduled body composition analysis evaluation
  • Nutrition and meal prep support
  • Meal plans
The PT or Coach will also provide support to keep you on track, stay focus and educate you on effective exercises to do and effective dietary changes. You can expect regular meet ups to track and review progress and to map out next steps to help you achieve your goals.
